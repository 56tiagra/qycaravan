About us

About us

We are the exclusive dealer for OurGen in Victoria. OurGen is a family owned and operated business in Melbourne, Australia. After over two decades’ experience working with recreational vehicles of every shape and size, we decided to do things differently.

GENPOD

The perfect introduction for beginner campers, our GenPod accommodates a Queen Bed, Fridge, Water Tank, and Gas Bottle Holder so your day-to-day routine can go on the road.

COMPLETE PERSONALISATION

We’re just getting started when it comes to customisation! OURGEN RV can work through a completely personalised RV with all the trimmings, fitted out exactly to your liking.
